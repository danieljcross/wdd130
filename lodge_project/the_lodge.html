Hello! We are the Cross family, and we would like to welcome you to our page!
We have decided to rent out our home for large multi-day events! This can include

Why should I stay here? Is this the right place for our group?

This is run completely by our family. You will find us to be very reasonable and down to earth. We want you to have a great trip, which is why we provide so many of these entertainment options. This really is a great house and in a great location.

There are many bedrooms, and tons of open gathering space. We ourselves bought it primarily for our own family gatherings. This is also a great place for work or school retreats. There are rooms both upstairs and downstairs which can allow for easy division for co-ed groups. If there is another reason you might have for staying here, let us know! We would love to accommodate for more needs.
